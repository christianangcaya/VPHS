Enhancing students' knowledge of the Filipino culture, Student Technologists and Entrepreneurs of the Philippines - Student Association (STEP-SA) and Kapisanan ng Wika ni Balagtas (𝐊𝐀𝐖𝐈𝐁𝐀), clubs of Vinzons Pilot High School (VPHS) joined forces as the celebration of Nutrition Month and Buwan ng Wika intertwined last September 8th, 2023.

Vinzons Pilot High School (VPHS) demonstrated unwavering commitment to earthquake preparedness after a successfully executed 3rd Quarter Nationwide Simultaneous Earthquake Drill (NSED) last September 7, 2023. With the help of the Bureau of Fire Protection Vinzons, a firetruck arrived at the campus at 4:09 p.m. which signaled the respondents in charge to prepare to start the simulation at 4:15 p.m. spearheaded by the School Disaster Risk Reduction Management Council (SDRRMC) President, John Erlov Cabezudo.

ICYMI: Gearing up for their future, aspiring Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) students from Vinzons Pilot High School (VPHS) completed a bridging program in Research and Statistics. The program lasted five days, from August 21 to 25, 2023 and was held in the Function Hall of VPHS. To ease curriculum delivery, the attendees were scheduled into two shifts: 8:00 am to 12:00 nn and 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm sessions. A total of 198 students from VPHS and transferees attended the said program.
